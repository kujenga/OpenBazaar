 5  """ Entangled DHT and distributed tuple space 
 6   
 7  The distributed hash table (DHT) used by Entangled is based on Kademlia, 
 8  and be accessed by the C{entangled.kademlia package}, or by simply 
 9  instantiating/subclassing the exposed C{KademliaNode} in the main C{entangled} 
10  package. 
11   
12  On top of this Kademlia node Entangled provides some extra functionality 
13  in the form of a "C{DELETE}" RPC and keyword-based search operations; these 
14  functions are accessible via the C{EntangledNode} class in the main 
15  C{entangled} package. 
16   
17  The Entangled distributed tuple space is exposed as the 
18  C{DistributedTupleSpacePeer} class, accessible via the main C{entangled} 
19  package or its C{dtuple} module. 
20  """
